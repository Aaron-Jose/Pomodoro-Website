This is a Pomodoro Timer
It helps with burnout and productivity
It alternates between study sessions, 
    short breaks and long breaks
A pomodoro refers to one session of work
Pomodoro sessions = 25 minutes.
Short breaks = 5 minutes.
Long breaks = 35 minutes.
Every Long break occurs after 4 consecutive
    work sessions eg. after 2h:25m
    this means that after every long break
    happens 3h hours have passed.
